Protecting People First: Health, Safety, and Emergency Response in Mining

HSSE is embedded across mining operations: PPE, traffic rules, blasting safety, emergency preparedness and health monitoring protect people and assets. HSSE systems include hazard identification and risk assessment, standard operating procedures, PPE policies, confined space and working-at-height controls, and emergency response plans (fire, spill, medical evacuation). Traffic management, speed limits and designated pedestrian routes reduce mobile equipment incidents. Routine drills and audits test readiness; health surveillance programmes monitor occupational exposures (respiratory, noise, vibration).
