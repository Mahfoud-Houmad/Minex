Handling the Byproducts: Tailings and Waste Management in Phosphate Mining

Engineered tailings systems store process residues safely while maximising water recovery and exploring valorisation options. TSFs are designed with engineered foundations, seepage barriers and progressive raising methods. Thickened tailings reduce water content and storage volume; decant systems collect return water for reuse. Tailings geotechnical monitoring (inclinometers, piezometers) provides early warning of instability; reclamation plans define progressive capping and revegetation. When feasible, fines are repurposed for building materials or soil amendments to reduce long-term storage needs
