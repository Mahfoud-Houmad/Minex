Moving phosphate from mines to farms and factories worldwide

Phosphate logistics ensures seamless movement of products from mines to end-users. Bulk carriers and rail transport raw ore to processing plants, while specialized trucks and ships deliver fertilizers globally. Strategic port terminals and storage facilities maintain supply chain efficiency. Temperature-controlled handling preserves product quality. This critical step bridges production with agriculture and industry worldwide.
