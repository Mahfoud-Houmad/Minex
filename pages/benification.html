Beneficiation

Transforming Raw Ore into High-Value Concentrate

Phosphate beneficiation upgrades raw ore by crushing, washing, and froth flotation to remove impurities, boosting P₂O₅ content. Optional acid leaching or calcination further purifies the ore, ending with filtration to produce a high-grade concentrate (28–34% P₂O₅) for fertilizers or chemicals.
