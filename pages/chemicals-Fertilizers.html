Transforming Phosphate into Essential Agricultural & Industrial Products

Beneficiated phosphate rock is chemically treated to create vital fertilizers (SSP, TSP, DAP/MAP) and industrial products. The core process uses sulfuric acid to produce phosphoric acid, which is then converted into agricultural nutrients or refined for food additives, detergents, and battery materials. This transformation turns raw ore into high-value products supporting global agriculture and industry
