Key Pillars of OCP’s Sustainability Strategy

Circular Resource Management

By embracing circular economy principles, OCP is closing the loop on phosphate use. We reintegrate waste streams back into processing cycles, recover phosphorus from wastewater, and continually optimize resource efficiency—an approach that minimizes waste and maximizes value across the entire life cycle of mining and manufacturing.

More

Aligned with the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals, OCP has developed a Circular Economy Program centered on four pillars: resource conservation, sustainable production, education for intelligent consumption, and value creation through efficient processing and recycling. This includes reintegrating low-phosphorus waste rock into beneficiation cycles and recovering phosphorus from wastewater and organic residues—a forward-thinking approach to resource stewardship.

Beyond waste reintegration, OCP is researching the transformation of byproducts such as gypsum into industrial materials like cement or agricultural soil amendments, minimizing waste and multiplying value streams. This initiative underscores how environmental responsibility can drive new economic opportunities across phosphate value chains.

Advanced Extraction & Beneficiation

OCP prioritizes efficiency from the ground up. Through precision beneficiation techniques—like high-performance washing and flotation—we extract more from every deposit, even lower-grade ore. This strategic refinement extends mine lifespan while reducing energy use and environmental impact.

More

OCP employs precision beneficiation techniques—including modern washing and flotation—to drastically improve the viability of lower-grade phosphate ores. These methods have turned previously uneconomical 33%-grade ores into productive assets while optimizing energy consumption and extraction efficacy.

The implementation of such technological advancements reinforces resource longevity, reduces environmental footprints, and enables OCP to meet global phosphate demand sustainably—without accelerating resource depletion or increasing ecological strain.

Green Energy & Carbon Neutrality

OCP is committed to powering its operations with clean energy. We're accelerating investments in solar, wind, and green ammonia production with the goal of achieving full carbon neutrality by 2040. This transition not only prevents greenhouse emissions but also supports Morocco’s broader sustainability goals.

More

Under its 2023–2027 Green Investment Program—a USD 12 billion initiative—OCP targets 100% renewable energy for all industrial operations by 2027 and full carbon neutrality by 2040. This comprehensive plan includes investments in solar, wind, hydroelectric power, green hydrogen, and green ammonia production.

OCP has secured partnerships for solar infrastructure, such as a €100 million green loan from IFC to build 202 MWp of solar PV in Benguerir and Khouribga, avoiding around 285,000 tons of CO₂ annually. With plans for vast seawater desalination and green ammonia plants, the group is redefining phosphate production through renewable-driven industrial transformation

Smart Farming & Tailored Nutrition

Through innovation in agronomy, OCP delivers precise, nutrient-rich fertilizers tailored to soil and crop needs. By optimizing dosing and reducing waste, we enhance yields while safeguarding the health of land and water—supporting farming systems that are productive and sustainable.

More

OCP’s sustainability philosophy extends to agriculture, where it champions precision fertilization. By customizing over 98 fertilizer formulations and embedding agronomic diagnostics, the company ensures farmers apply only what’s needed—enhancing yields while preserving soils and reducing environmental runoff.

Their R&D collaboration with Mohammed VI Polytechnic University (UM6P) drives innovation in sustainable agriculture, including remote sensing, soil fertility research, and smart irrigation. OCP also trains farmers across Africa, equipping them with knowledge to adopt smarter, eco-friendly practices that secure productivity and preserve resources.

OCP’s Green Investment & Energy Transition

100% Renewable Energy by 2027

OCP aims to power all its industrial facilities using renewable energy from wind, solar, hydroelectric, and cogeneration sources by 2027. By 2040, the Group is targeting full carbon neutrality, including coverage of Scopes 1, 2, and 3 emissions.

Green Ammonia Plan Investment

As part of its green energy strategy, OCP is investing USD 7 billion to build a renewable-powered green ammonia plant in Tarfaya. Production is expected to start at 200,000 tonnes per year by 2026, ramp up to 1 million by 2027, and reach 3 million tonnes by 2032.

Circular Economy Strategy

OCP’s Green Investment Program (2023–2027) is built upon a circular-economy approach—recycling waste, preserving phosphate resources, promoting sustainable production, advancing recovery from mining residues, and fostering education for smarter use.
